From Expert to Global Authority: Building the Digital Presence of a United Nations Consultant

CLIENT:

Dr. Chijioke J. Evoh

DURATION:

February 2026 – Present

ROLE:

Digital Strategy, Website Development & Authority Positioning

INDUSTRY:

Global Policy, Development, Multilateral Advisory

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Dr. Chijioke J. Evoh operates at the highest levels of global development policy, working with institutions such as the United Nations, International Labour Organization (ILO), and multiple governments across Africa, Europe, and beyond

Despite extensive experience, publications, and international engagements, his digital footprint did not yet reflect the depth, scale, and authority of his work.

The objective was clear:

Transform his presence into a globally recognized digital authority platform — one that reinforces credibility, increases visibility, and positions him as a go-to expert in policy, sustainable livelihoods, and employment systems.

OBJECTIVES

  • Position Dr. Evoh as a globally recognized authority in policy, livelihoods, and employment systems
  • Build a digital presence that reflects institutional-level credibility and expertise.
  • Increase discoverability across search engines, academic, and policy ecosystems.
  • Establish a foundation for thought leadership, citations, and global recognition.
  • Create a structured platform to support speaking, consulting, and advisory opportunities

The Challenges

  • Under-leveraged Digital Presence : Extensive global experience existed, but was not fully translated into a structured, authoritative online footprint
  • Fragmented Visibility : Publications, policy work, and institutional contributions were scattered across platforms, reducing discoverability
  • High-Level Audience Expectations : Target audience includes governments, multilateral agencies, and global institutions — requiring a premium, credibility-first presentation
  • Authority vs Visibility Gap : Real-world authority was high, but digital authority signals (SEO, structured content, discoverability) were underdeveloped

Core Strategy

The Strategy was divided in 5 phases: Foundation & Positioning, Execution & Development, Authority Structuring and Authority Building & Global Integration (Ongoing)

Foundation & Positioning

  • Deep audit of Dr. Evoh’s career, publications, and institutional engagements
  • Structured his expertise into clear authority pillars:
    • Employment Policy
    • Sustainable Livelihoods
    • Private Sector Development
    • Labour Migration & Development
  • Defined positioning narrative: Global Policy Expert. Research-Driven. Institution-Level Impact

Execution & Development

  • Broke strategy into focused component
  • Set timelines & deliverables

Website Design & Development

  • Designed and developed a high-authority website.
  • Structured content to reflect:
    • Depth of experience (15+ years across multiple countries)
    • Institutional credibility (UNDP, ILO, UNICEF engagements)
    • Research authority (publications, policy frameworks, reports)
  • Built a clear narrative hierarchy:
    Authority → Experience → Impact → Thought Leadership

Digital Strategy Development(Ongoing)

  • Search Engine Optimization Targeting high-intent keywords across:Employment Policy, Sustainable Livelihoods and Development Strategy
  • Technical SEO optimization for discoverability.
  • Content expansion for long-term ranking and authority
  • Tracked performance via Google Analytics, refining strategy based on results.

Authority Structuring

  • Organized publications, reports, and policy work into structured, indexable formats.
  • Highlighted high-impact contributions:
    • National Employment Policy development
    • Decent Work Country Programmes (multiple countries).
    • Post-crisis recovery strategies (Ebola, COVID-19, Libya)
  • Profile Positioning as a policy architect, not just contributor

Authority Building & Global Integration (Ongoing)

  • Global directory submissions (policy, research, academic platforms)
  • Integration into knowledge ecosystems (Google Scholar, institutional references, etc.)
  • Expansion of digital footprint across:
    • Publications
    • Citations
    • Thought leadership platforms

KEY OUTCOMES & RESULTS

Since launch (February 2026):

  • Established a centralized digital authority platform
  • Created a clear and structured representation of 15+ years of global policy work
  • Positioned Dr. Evoh for increased global visibility and recognition
  • Built the foundation for:
    • Search engine dominance in niche policy areas
    • Increased consulting and advisory opportunities
    • Thought leadership expansion

CLIENT COLLABORATION & VALIDATION

Real-time client feedback, strategic alignment, and validated execution across the project lifecycle.

Strategic Collaboration & Refinement

Throughout execution, the project evolved through direct client input and high-level strategic alignment. Key refinements included integrating Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E) into the positioning, and structuring country-level engagements such as Ghana and Sierra Leone’s Decent Work Country Programmes (DWCPs).

"...I will appreciate it if you can capture my research consultant assignments for the governments of Ghana and Sierra Leone in their Decent Work Country Programs (DWCPs). Thanks"
